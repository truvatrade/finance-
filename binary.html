What is Binary Options Trading?

Binary Options, also known as Digital Options, were introduced in 2008 as a simplified way to trade global financial markets. They allow traders to profit by predicting the direction of an asset's price over a fixed time. You don’t need to own the asset—just correctly forecast whether its price will go up or down.

Binary Options are ideal for both beginners and experienced traders due to their simplicity. You can trade assets like currencies, indices, commodities, or stocks. All you do is select an asset, set an expiry time, and decide whether the price will be higher or lower than your entry point when the time is up.

How to Trade Binary Options on Online Tasty Trade

  • Choose the asset you want to trade.
  • Select "Call" if you think the price will rise above your entry point at expiry.
  • Select "Put" if you think the price will fall below your entry point at expiry.
  • Wait for the trade to expire and see the result.
  • If correct, earn a fixed return; if incorrect, the trade ends out-of-the-money.

Advantages of Trading Binary Options

  • Straightforward trading — predict direction, choose time, and trade.
  • Fixed risk and reward — no hidden spreads, commissions, or fees.
  • Trade 24/7 — markets are always open across global assets.
  • Fully customizable — you choose the asset, expiry time, and investment amount.
  • Even small price moves can lead to profit — just one point in your favor is enough.
